FEATURES

Register Boat

Newly and unfamiliarized boats need to be registered. This includes the boat's serial number, boat's name, types of items (e.g. electronics, food supplies) and list of items (e.g. rice, fish).

Monitor Boat

This feature can monitor the boat present in the area with its ID number and serial number to avoid overcrowding on the port. It can also monitor the arrival and departure time of each boat.
